Hireology 3.0

HR & Recruitment Platform

View Demo

Hireology is an applicant tracking system running solely to help companies hire their best teams. After joining the UX team and digging into some user data and customer feedback, it became clear our app was creating some significant challenges for our users. Our UX team was dedicated to pitching a new direction for our app and after research and many conversations with stakeholders we got the green light to move forward with this vision as a company. 3.0 was a long-running dreamed up conception and vision that hit the ground running December 2022.

I am so proud of the work we put into the new app and thrilled to be able to see the barebones come to life after so many months of discussing and dreaming up our new ATS platform.

Problems 3.0 is solving:
  • Centralized location for Settings and Permissions within the app.
  • Implementing a job/people pipeline will give users a high level status view of candidates within the hiring process.
  • Jobs model redefined. Roles are templates used at an account level that can be used to open jobs accross locations and divisions. This will reduce duplication in jobs and help companies better track the health and lifespan of their open jobs.
  • Easier management of users and their established roles accross multiple companies.
  • Data has shown a higher user percentage. 3.0's mobile experience is prioritized at every point within the app.
  • Inbox and messaging platform enhanced within the app. Users are able to manage tasks and notifcations and communicate seemlessly with applicants and candidates.
