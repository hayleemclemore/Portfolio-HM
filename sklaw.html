SK Law

Back to Portfolio

Fully responsive web design for SK Law, a highly valued personal injury law firm serving both New York and New Jersey injury victims.

After an initial creative brief with the client, I was able to gather several goals for their new site. He stated he want more organization, micro-interactions and updated visuals of the firm. I sat down and researched some competitors and put together a collection of ideas I had found. I then was able to collaborate with a copywriter and begin wireframing the layout and navigation. I was in constant communication with a developer to ensure my ideas could be built out as I had envisioned. The next stage led to further client conversations and approval on initial mock-ups before fully designing internal pages.

Case Results Feature

The client made it clear to us they wanted to highlight case results and settlement values on their site. This information is valuable to the firm and communicates success and win-win relationships with clients. I designed a toggle feature to display the case results for SK Law and worked with a developer to see it come to life. The client was thrilled with the functionality and attention it was given on their new site.

At this time, the owners have parted ways and the firm is now longer active as SK Law.
