Collocation Creation

An online ESL resource.

Our project was inspired by the need for more online study resources for English Language learners worldwide. It combines a dictionary, pronunciation app, a thesaurus and a much needed resource to show collocations, e.g. words more than randomly paired the word searched.

The motivation for this project came from English as a Second Language students not having a good resource to go to in order to further their English education. Resources for learning English initially are plentiful, but when it came to a certain point, the resources dried up. We wanted to fill that gap to be able to support ESL students.
